Summer camps are the heart of summer break for kids and their parents. AllPlayers.com takes the hassle out of summer camp management, registration and communication, so you can spend more time teaching, leading and having fun!

Online Registration

Maintain your registration process 24/7 by encouraging busy parents to enroll their children online. Parents who have to go out of their way to enroll, or print and mail documents for registration, will be less inclined to register with your camp; they'll look for one with convenient online registration.

How many times have you had to mail or phone a parent to remind them of an outstanding payment? AllPlayers.com takes that off your plate and enables instant credit card payment online at the time of registration. Of course, you can still collect fees in-house, or enable registration without payment for individuals.

Installment payment plans

Make it easy for your campers to afford and pay for your camp. AllPlayers.com gives you the option of setting up installment payment plans for your campers.

Group administrators can define payment plans, including first and final payment dates, enabling participants to pay gradually over a defined time period.

Paperwork reduction

Repetitive paperwork is a drag. Ask any parent who enrolls their children in more than one camp or activity and they'll all tell you they wish there was an easier way! With AllPlayers.com, you have the answer they want.

Parents can complete registration documents online, sign waivers, read handbooks and agree to camp rules all online at their convenience. And the best thing? They only have to do it once per child! As long as the camper's information doesn't change, the same paperwork is stored for your records.

Instant Communication

Change in schedule? Field trip canceled the morning before camp? AllPlayers.com allows you to reach parents and individuals in an instant through email or SMS text message with the information.

Reminders for lunches or special attire can be sent immediately. Busy parents appreciate this benefit, and know they can count on you to relay important information when it is needed.

Bring the camp home

Create a page just for your camp, or for individual groups within your camp for parents, friends and instructors to keep up to date with photos, news and more. Each evening parents can log in to see what is going on the next day or week at camp.

Create photo albums so parents can view photos from last week's field trip. Parents can comment on activities, contact the counselors/leaders and become more active and knowledgeable in their children's daily routines.
