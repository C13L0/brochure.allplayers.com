Stay in contact with your group members through text or email messages. Members decide how they want to receive your updates and we deliver your message according to their settings.

Perfect for small groups of individuals as well as large multi-group organizations. Send your messages to everyone in one group, or to some people in some groups. You can even pick and choose certain roles within each group to target your communications to specific participants.
