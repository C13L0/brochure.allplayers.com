Share group memories with all of your group mates by creating and sharing group photo albums.

There is always that one wonderful person at each event who not only brings their camera but actually remembers to get those snapshots off their data card. Thankfully for the rest of us, those wonderful photographers can upload their photos and share them with the rest of us so we can go back and relive the moments!
