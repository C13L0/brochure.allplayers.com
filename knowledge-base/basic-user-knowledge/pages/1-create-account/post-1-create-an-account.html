In order to join a group or create your own group at AllPlayers.com, you will need to create an account. Registration is free and takes only a few minutes to complete.

From the AllPlayers.com home page, click Create account.

Complete the form

Complete the form, then click Create new account.

Complete the profile wizard

After completing the form, you will be redirected to your new user home page.

A dialog box will pop up on your screen, asking you a few questions to verify your information and account settings.

Proceed through the screens:

  1. Verify that your profile information is correct.
  2. Choose your local time zone. If in doubt, choose the time zone closest to your location with the same rules for daylight saving time.
  3. Enter your mobile phone number if you would like to receive notifications on your mobile phone.
  4. Review your information and when you're done, click Finish.

You can close this dialog box if you would like to complete this information at a later time. The dialog box will pop up each day that you visit to remind you to complete this process.

Verify your account

On your new home page, you will see a message regarding account verification. In order to gain access to many of the features on our site, you must verify your account.

You do not need to be verified in order to register with an organization.

To verify your account, an email was sent to you from AllPlayers.com. If you do not see this email in your inbox, check your spam folder. This message contains a link you will click to verify your account.

Click on the link in the email and the AllPlayers.com website will open in your browser with your account verified. That's it!

If you can't locate your account verification email, you may resend the account verification email by clicking the resend the verification email link that displays on your page in the Account Verification message.
