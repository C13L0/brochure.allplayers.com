As a visitor or registered user at AllPlayers.com, you can find a group through the Find a group search tool.

While on any page, click Find a group in your top navigation menu.

Filter your search

Enter the options that you would like to use as your search parameters:

  • Search: Enter the name of an organization or keywords to return search results.
  • Postal code: Enter your postal/zip code to find groups near you.
  • Distance: Specify the distance from your postal code to find groups within a specific radius of your postal code.
  • Category: Narrow your search results by category.
  • Registration status: Narrow your search results to show all groups or only groups with open registration.

Once you have selected the search parameters you would like to use, click Search

The Category and Registration status options can be found by clicking the Advanced Options button.

View your search results

Your search will return results based on your entered parameters.

To narrow or widen your results, modify your search parameters and click Search.
