Groups on AllPlayers.com are designed to feature an organization's activities for their members. A group can be anything from a camp, to a tryout, to a team page.

As a visitor or registered user at AllPlayers.com, you can find a group through our Find a group search tool.

Click Find a group in your top navigation menu.

If you do not have an AllPlayers.com account, you can still follow these same steps to search for a group.

Fill out search parameters

Enter the options that you would like to use as your search parameters, then click Search.

  • Search: Enter the name of an organization or keywords to return search results.
  • Postal code: Enter your postal/zip code to find groups near you.
  • Distance: Specify the distance from your postal code to find groups within a specific radius of your postal code.
  • Category: Narrow your search results by category.
  • Registration status: Select whether to show all groups, or only those with open registration.

The Category and Registration status options can be found by clicking Advanced Options.

View your search results

Your search will return results based on your entered parameters.

You can sort your results by City, State, or Zip by clicking on the headers of their respective columns.

To narrow or widen your results, modify your search parameters and click Search.
