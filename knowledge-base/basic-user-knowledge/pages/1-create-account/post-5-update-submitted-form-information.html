After you submit a form on AllPlayers.com, updating the form with new information is quick and easy.

If you are on your home page, click Submitted Forms in your Account Settings box.

If you are on any other page, you can click Submitted Forms in the Account Settings dropdown in your navigation bar.


Click Edit next to the form that you would like to update.

You can also update your children's submitted forms from this page.


Update the information that needs to be changed. Click Submit, and you're done!
