Guardians of children aged 13 and under have the ability to Act on behalf of their children to control what features are available to their children, including viewing friends and messages.

Click the AllPlayers.com logo or Home button at the top of any page to go to your home page.

Edit child's My Information page

In the Friends and Family box on your home page, you can access your child's My Information page to edit their information.

Select the Family tab.


Click edit next to the name of the child whose profile you wish to edit.


You are now on your child's My Information page where you can edit information about them.

Edit other aspects of child's account.

To update other information on your child's account, you will need to log in as them.

To do this, either log out of your account and login with your child's account information, or use the Act on behalf feature to masquerade as them.

You do not need to login as your child to register them for a group or to make a payment on your child's account.

Start at the family box on your homepage.


Click the name of the child who you would like to act on behalf of.


Click the Act on behalf of this user button.


You will now be acting on behalf of your child and will be sent to their homepage.

Update all of the information that you need to update on your child's account.


Once you have finished acting on behalf of your child, don't forget to log out and log back in with your own account information.
