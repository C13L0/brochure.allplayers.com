When your account is created, you either chose a password or a password was generated for you. If you need to change this password, you can do so from the Change Password page.

If you are on your home page, click Change Password in your Account Settings box.

If you are on any other page, you can click Change Password in the Account Settings dropdown in your navigation bar.

Enter your new password in the New password and Confirm new password box.

Click Change Password to save your changes.
