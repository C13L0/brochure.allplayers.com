You can add your child to your account at AllPlayers.com during the group registration process.

As your child's parent/guardian at AllPlayers.com, you are able to monitor your child's activities on AllPlayers.com. You will automatically be added as a guardian to any group your child joins, so you can receive updates and view group events in your calendar.

To add a child to your account during group registration:

Click the Register or Join button for the group you want to join. This may be from the group's front page or another page at AllPlayers.com.


In the registration form select My Child.


If you do not yet have a child on AllPlayers.com, you will be brought to a form to input your child's information.

If you already have a child on AllPlayers.com, click add child to add another child to your account.


Input the form with your child's information, then click Add child and continue.

NOTE: If your child does not have their own email address, mail intended for your child will be forwarded to your email address.


Your child has been added to your account. Select the role you wish to register your child for, and continue with the group registration process.
