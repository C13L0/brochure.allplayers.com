After your child's account has been created, you may wish to link additional parents/guardians to your child's account to share guardian privileges, calendars and other site features. If the additional parent/guardian already has an AllPlayers.com account, follow the steps below to link them to your child's account.

×

These instructions are only for adding parent/guardians with existing AllPlayers.com accounts to existing child accounts. If you wish to add a parent/guardian who does not have an account to your child's account, follow the instructions here.

To invite a user with an AllPlayers.com account to become a child's guardian:

Click the AllPlayers.com logo or Home button at the top of any page to go to your home page.


On your home page, find the Family box. Click your child's name.

If you are unable to click your child's name, your account is unverified. You must verify your account before continuing.


You should now be on the child's profile page. Click Act on behalf of this user in the left column of the page.


You are now acting on behalf of your child, allowing you to modify this child's account.

When you are acting on behalf of your child, you will see a notice at the bottom of the screen.


In the Family box, click Add Family and select Invite Parent/guardian from the drop down menu.


Enter the email(s) of the parent/guardians you wish to link to this child's account as a guardian.

You may also include a message that will be added to the email sent to the person you are inviting.


Click Send now.

An email will be sent to the email address(es) you entered. The recipient(s) should follow instructions in the email to become a parent/guardian for this child on AllPlayers.com.
