AllPlayers.com will alert you via email or SMS text message when you have messages from our system. You can choose to receive the messages via email or SMS text message to your mobile device. In addition, you can choose how often you want those messages delivered to you.

  • Activate your device to receive SMS text notifications
  • Subscribe to receive SMS text notifications from a group
