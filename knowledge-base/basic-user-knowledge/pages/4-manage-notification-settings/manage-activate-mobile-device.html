If you are on your home page, click Notification Settings in your Account Settings box.

If you are on any other page, you can hover over Account Settings in your navigation bar and then select Notification Settings in the dropdown.

To receive SMS text notifications from AllPlayers.com on your mobile device, that device needs to be activated by following the instructions below.

Click the AllPlayers.com logo or Home button at the top of any page to go to your home page.

Click Account in your side menu, then click Notification settings in the submenu.


Click Administer your mobile settings.


Enter your mobile phone number, and select your mobile carrier. If your carrier is not listed, contact AllPlayers.com support at support@allplayers.com.


Click Add phone number.


Check your mobile device. You will receive a text message from AllPlayers.com containing a 4-digit code.

Enter this code and click Confirm Number.

You will now be able to receive text messages from your AllPlayers.com groups on your mobile device.
