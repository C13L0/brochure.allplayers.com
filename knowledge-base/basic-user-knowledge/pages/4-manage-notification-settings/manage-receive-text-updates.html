Once you have activated your mobile device to receive SMS text updates, you can choose to receive text updates from any group you are a member of by following the steps below.

Before you can receive SMS text updates, you must activate your mobile device. Click here for instructions.


If you are on your home page, click Notification Settings in your Account Settings box.

If you are on any other page, you can hover over Account Settings in your navigation bar and then select Notification Settings in the dropdown.


Click Create a group subscription


Select the group you would like to subscribe to from the drop down list.

Choose the type of update you would like to receive.

Select SMS from the next drop down.

Click Add once you have made all of your selections. That’s it! Now you will receive updates from your group while you’re on the go.
