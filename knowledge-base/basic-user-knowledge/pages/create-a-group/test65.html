A group can be an organization, school, recreational business, league, team, family... the possibilities are endless! At any time, you can add groups below or above your group. For example, an organization might have leagues made up of teams. Each organization, league and team is a separate group. The league has a group above (the organization) and groups below (the teams.)

To create a new group, log in to your account or create a new account.

Then, in the upper menu bar, go to Group > Create Group.


Complete the information requested on the Create Group page.


Group Name: The name of your organization. Your group page URL will be automatically created based on the name of your group.

Search Terms: Displayed to visitors searching for activities.

Location: Your main address. Only the ZIP code field is required; however, if your group has a physical location, we recommend entering it to help your members find you.

Category Info: Choose the category that best describes your group. This will help customize your group page with information relevant to your members. Site visitors can also search for groups by category.

×

If you are creating a team, we suggest adding your main Group Above in the Search Terms field to help visitors find you. For example, if your Group Name is Eagles (the name of your team), and your team is part of the Springfield Rec Center Winter 2011 Basketball League, include Springfield Rec Center Winter 2011 Basketball League in this field.
