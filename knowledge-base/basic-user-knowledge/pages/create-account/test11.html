When your account is created, you either chose a password or a password was generated for you. If you need to change this password, you can do so from the Change Password page.

Click the AllPlayers.com logo or Home button at the top of any page to go to your home page.


Click Account in your side navigation menu, then click Change Password in the submenu.


Enter your new password in the Change Password and Confirm Password box.

Click Change Password to save your changes.
