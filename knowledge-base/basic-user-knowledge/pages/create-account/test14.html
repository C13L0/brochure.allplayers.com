Once you have activated your mobile device to receive SMS text updates, you can choose to receive text updates from any group you are a member of by following the steps below.

x

Before you can receive SMS text updates, you must activate your mobile device. Click here for instructions.

Click the AllPlayers.com logo or Home button at the top of any page to go to your home page.


Click on Account in your side menu, then click Notification settings in the submenu.


Click Advanced Settings to show more options, then select Content type in group.


Select the group you would like to subscribe to from the drop down list. Choose the type of update you would like to receive. Select SMS from the next drop down.


Click Add once you have made all of your selections. That’s it! Now you will receive updates from your group while you’re on the go.
