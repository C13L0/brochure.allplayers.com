You can add your spouse/co-guardian to your account at AllPlayers.com through your home page. A co-guardian is any adult that should have guardian privileges for your child's account. If your spouse/co-guardian already has an AllPlayers.com account, you can invite them to become a guardian for your child.

Since the account for the spouse/co-guardian provides guardian privileges to your children's accounts, you should create and link your children's accounts before adding your spouse/co-guardian.

To add a spouse/co-guardian to your account:

Click the AllPlayers.com logo or Home button at the top of any page to go to your home page.

On your home page, find the Family box. Click Add Family at the bottom, then select Add Spouse/co-guardian from the drop-down menu.

If you do not have children linked to your account, you will not be able to create an account for your spouse/co-guardian. To create an account for your child, follow the instructions here.

Select which child or children your spouse/co-guardian should have guardian access for.

Complete the form with your spouse/co-guardian's information.

Click Create new account.

You will receive confirmation that your spouse/co-guardian's account has been created. Your spouse/co-guardian will now appear in your Family box and your Friends box on your Home page.
