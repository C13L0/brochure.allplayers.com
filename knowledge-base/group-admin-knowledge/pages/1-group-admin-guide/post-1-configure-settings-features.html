When you create your group, you can choose to enable features and settings that your group may find helpful.

AllPlayers.com is constantly adding new features suggested by our site users, so you may see additional feature options that aren't yet listed here.

Settings

Active activates the group when you complete your Create Group form.

Fee (Approval Required) submits a request for approval to accept online registration payments for your group. If you would like your group members to be able to register and pay for your group through AllPlayers.com, turn this ON. Your request will automatically be sent to membership@allplayers.com.

Group Mates allows members of your group to have a relationship with one another, so they can see each other's profiles and send messages to one another while the group is active.

Groups Above is used to establish a relationship between groups. For example, if you are creating groups that are teams, and these teams belong to a league, the league is the group above the team.

At any time, you can adjust the features or settings of your group by going to your group's home page, then using your Group Toolbox to select More Tools > Features or More Tools > Group Settings.

Features

Auto Schedule Events allows you to automatically create league game schedules, based on teams and resources you assign to the group.

Calendar allows you to create and post events to your group. Your group page will display a calendar, and your events will automatically populate in your members' personal calendars. TIP: You should enable Calendar if you plan to use the Auto Schedule Events feature.

Downloads allows you to easily add downloadable files, such as PDF forms or files you need to distribute to your members, to your group pages.

Photos allows you to create photo albums and upload photos to your group pages.

Store allows you to accept registration fees and donations online. Your group must be pre-approved to accept online payments. For more information, please email orders@allplayers.com.

Tournament Bracketsallows you to create a wide variety of tournament brackets, enter scores and automatically advance teams through a tournament.

Additional Options

The last step to create your group is to configure the colors and logo for your group. Choose two colors that complement your group's colors, and upload a logo or image for your group.

You're Done!

Click Create Group when you're ready, and you'll be taken directly to your new group home page.
