If your group has been approved to accept payments through AllPlayers.com, you can accept donations and contributions from your AllPlayers.com group pages.

Display donations box on your page

Click Design Page with Dashboard in your Group Toolbox to show your Dashboard.

Choose Group Info from the Add dropdown list.

Click and drag the Send Payment box onto your page.

See Add boxes to your page for more information on adding elements to your page with your dashboard.

When you have placed the Send Payment box on your page, click Save Changes in your Dashboard.

If you would like to change where you have placed your box, see Move boxes around on your page for more information.

Customize your box

Navigate to the group for which you would like to accept payments.

Select More Tools > Bills in your Group Toolbox.

Select Billing Settings in your group submenu.

Enter the text you wish to appear in your Send Payment Settings block that will display on your group pages.

Save your settings.

Your Send Payments box should now show the custom text that you configured.
