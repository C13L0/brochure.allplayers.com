AllPlayers.com allows groups to apply to accept payments online through AllPlayers.com.

Some common uses of this feature:

  • Accept payment at the time of registration for sports leagues and teams
  • Allow members to make a donation to your organization
  • Create groups for events and tournaments, and accept payments when members register for the event

Navigate to the group that you would like members to join.

Select More Tools > Registration in your Group Toolbox.

Scroll to the Settings section.

Check the Fee (Approval Required) box to request the ability to receive payments online.

Choose the Payee if options exist.

×

Payee is the organization that will receive any payments or fees that are made to your group.

If you are the group administrator for other groups that have already been approved for payment, those groups will appear under Payee. If the payments received by the group you are configuring should be paid to an existing Payee, select that Payee.

If you are not an administrator for other groups that have already been approved for payment, None will appear. Select None, then return to this page to update the Payee after your application has been approved.

It is your responsibility to select the correct payee for each of your groups.

Scroll to the bottom of the page and click Save Group.

After saving, you will receive confirmation that your request to accept fees has been received by AllPlayers.com.

Typically within 1-2 business days, you should receive an email from AllPlayers.com that includes a forms packet that you will need to complete.

Fax completed forms to AllPlayers.com at (469) 941-0590.

You may also download the forms packet here.

When your packet has been reviewed and approved by AllPlayers.com, you will receive a Letter of Agreement that requires your review and signature.

Execute and fax Letter of Agreement to AllPlayers.com at (469) 941-0590.

When your Letter of Agreement is received by AllPlayers.com, you will receive a confirmation email from AllPlayers.com informing you that you are now able to accept credit card payments from your members online at AllPlayers.com.

Return to AllPlayers.com to select your Payee now that you are approved to accept payments.

After you have been approved to accept payments

Navigate to the group from which you would like to accept payments.

Select More Tools > Group Settings in your Group Toolbox.

Your newly approved group will appear under Payee. Choose this group.

Scroll to the bottom of the page and click Save Group.
