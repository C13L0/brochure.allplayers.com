As a group admin, you can upload electronic files to your group pages that your members can download. Your files might be forms that your members should complete, a PDF of your league rulebook, end-of-season parent evaluations, or whatever you may need to distribute to your team members.

×

Downloads is a group feature that you can enable or disable based on your needs. Before beginning, ensure that Downloads is enabled by visiting More Tools > Features in your Group Toolbox.

When you click Design Page with Dashboard in your Group Toolbox, your Dashboard will appear within your browser window.

Choose Downloads from the Add dropdown list.

Click and drag the File Downloads box onto your page.

See Add boxes to your page for more information on adding elements to your page with your Dashboard.

When you have placed the File Downloads box on your page, click Save Changes in your Dashboard.

If you would like to change where you have placed your box, see Move boxes around on your page for more information.

Now it's time to add your file. Select Upload a File in your Group Toolbox.

Complete the requested fields.

Typically within 1-2 business days, you should receive an email from AllPlayers.com that includes a forms packet that you will need to complete.

Title: Enter the name of your document that will display to your members.

File: Click Choose file to select the file you wish to add from your computer, then click Upload. Please note the allowed file types and maximum file size.

Groups: Select the groups for which you would like you file to be available. NOTE: To display your file on the group pages, the File Downloads box must be enabled for each group via your Dashboard.

When you've completed the form, click Save.

After you have been approved to accept payments

Navigate to the group from which you would like to accept payments.

Select More Tools > Group Settings in your Group Toolbox.

Your new file will now appear in your File Downloads box on your group page. You can edit or remove your file at any time by clicking the Edit link, which only group administrators will see.
