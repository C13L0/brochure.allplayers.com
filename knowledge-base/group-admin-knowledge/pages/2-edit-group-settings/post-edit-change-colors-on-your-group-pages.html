To change your group logo, click More Tools > Group Settings in your Group Toolbox.


Scroll to the Additional Options section of your Edit Group Settings page.


When you move your mouse over the color swatch for Primary Color or Secondary Color, a color palette will appear.

Choose your colors.


When you move your mouse off the color palettes, you will see the colors that are currently selected.


Save your changes.


You will see your new colors on your group pages.


×

The AllPlayers.com default website colors (dark blue and dark red) can be used by selecting black as both your Primary Color and Secondary Color. If you would like to use blacks in your color palette, we recommend using one of the shades of gray that are available in your color selections.
