When you are logged in as an administrator for a group, you have access to buttons and links in your Group Toolbox that other group members will not see.

These tools allow you to manage, create and customize your group pages.

To customize your group page, click Design Page with Dashboard. The Dashboard allows you to:

  • Change the layout of the group page
  • Add automatically generated boxes to the group page
  • Remove boxes from the group page
  • Create your own custom boxes for the group page
  • Move boxes from one location to another on the group page
