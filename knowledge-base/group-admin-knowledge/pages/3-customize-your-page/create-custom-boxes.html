In addition to the boxes that AllPlayers.com provides for your group, you can create custom boxes to communicate to your group. Custom boxes are perfect for showing announcements, stories, sponsors, and whatever else you want to share with your group. Content in your custom boxes will display to all visitors to your group page.

To create a custom box, open your Dashboard by clicking Design Page with Dashboard in your Group Toolbox.

In the Add dropdown, select Boxes.

Drag and drop the Add custom box box to the area of your page where you want your new box to live.

Complete the requested fields to populate your new custom box with content.

Box description: This will only show up in your Dashboardto identify the custom box. We recommend keeping it short but descriptive enough to identify the box in your Dashboard.

Box title: This title will appear on your page as the headline for your new box.

Box body: This is the content of your box. You may use HTML tags in your content. For more information on allowed tags, visit our Filtered HTML Formatting Tips page.

When you have added your text, click the Save for your new block. You'll see a preview of your entry. You may edit your block by clicking the Edit link.

If you have no additional changes to your block, click Save Changes in your Dashboard to finish your new box.

As a group admin, you can edit your custom boxes at any time by clicking the Edit link that appears at the bottom of your custom box. Your dashboard doesn't need to be open to edit an existing box. Just click Save when you finish your edits and your custom box changes will be saved. You may need to refresh your browser window to see the proper styling applied to the box.
