In addition to controlling your page layout, the Dashboard allows you to customize your page with additional boxes to provide more information to your group.

Dashboard by clicking Design Page with Dashboard in your Group Toolbox.

Use the Add controls to add boxes to your page.

For this example, we'll add a Calendar to our page.

Select Add > Calendar in your Dashboard.

You'll notice that each element category has pre-made boxes that you can enable on your group page. We'll add a Mini Calendar to page, and place it in the left sidebar.

When you move your cursor over the Mini Calendar box, your cursor changes to the hand icon.

Click and drag the Mini Calendar box to where you would like to place it on your page.

When the box is in a location where you can place it, a grey dashed line appears.

Release your mouse to drop the box where you would like to place it.

Your new box is now in place. Your changes will be saved and styled when you click Save Changes in your Dashboard.

×

While you are customizing your page, leave your Dashboard window open. You can add or remove many boxes at once, and move them around your page, as long as your Dashboard is open. Once you finish customizing your page, click the Save Changes button to save and apply all of your changes.
