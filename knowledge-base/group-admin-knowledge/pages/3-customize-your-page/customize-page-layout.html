When you click Design Page with Dashboard, your Dashboard will appear within your browser window.

The Dashboard allows you to change the layout of your group page. It also allows you to customize your page with additional boxes to provide more information to your group.

Use the Layout controls to change the layout of your group page.

Site Default sets your page layout to three columns, with sidebars on the left and right of the page, and a content area in the center.

Left Sidebar sets your page layout to two columns, with a sidebar on the left and a content area filling the remainder of the page.

Right Sidebar sets your page layout to two columns, with a sidebar on the right and a content area filling the remainder of the page.

2 columns sets your page layout to two columns, with both columns equal in size.

×

Your Dashboard is draggable within your browser window. Place your cursor over the grey title bar in the Dashboard and your cursor will change to the drag icon, indicating that you can then move your Dashboard anywhere within your window.

Once you've changed your layout option, click Save. Your Dashboard will close and you'll see your new layout.
