You can move your boxes to any column on your page, or within a column, through your Dashboard.

To move boxes around your page, open your Dashboard by clicking Design Page with Dashboard in your Group Toolbox.

With your Dashboard open, move your cursor over boxes on your page. As your cursor goes over a box, controls will appear in the upper right corner of the boxes.

To move your box, mouse over the drag icon. Your cursor will change to the hand icon, and you can drag your box to a new position on the page.

When your box is in a position where you can drop it, you'll see a dashed grey line on your page. Release your mouse to drop the box into its new position.

Click Save Changes in your Dashboard when you are satisfied with your changes.
