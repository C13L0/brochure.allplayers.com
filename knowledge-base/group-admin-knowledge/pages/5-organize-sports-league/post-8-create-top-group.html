When you've created your organizational chart, you're ready to begin building your groups at AllPlayers.com. See Organize a sports league for our suggestions on how to build an organizational chart to maintain your leagues, divisions and teams moving forward through seasons.

In this guide, we'll use our sample organizational chart for the fictional Springfield Recreation Center to demonstrate how you can easily create groups.

If your organization is new to AllPlayers.com, create your top group first.

Create your top group

In our example, the top group is Springfield Recreation Center.

Log in to your account or create a new account.

In the upper menu bar, go to Group > Create Group.

Complete the requested information, then click Create Group when you are finished.

For full details on creating a group, see Create your group and Configure settings and features.

After you click Create Group, you'll be taken directly to your new group home page.
