As you create your groups within your organization, you may need to create additional, similar groups.

For example, in our sample organizational chart, we have five groups under our Sports Programs group: Football, Baseball, Basketball, Soccer and Volleyball.

After creating one group like Soccer, we can quickly duplicate that group to create the remaining groups.

From the front page that will be the Group Above your new group, go to More Tools in yourGroup Toolbox and choose Subgroup.

To create the group to be copied, follow the steps to Create a Subgroup.

Once you have created the first group that you want to duplicate, go to More Tools in your Group Toolbox and choose Subgroup, to create your next group.

On the Create Subgroup page, find the group you want to copy under Create a copy of an existing group and select Create a Copy of this Group.

Your new Create Subgroup form will be pre-populated with all of the information from the group you are duplicating, except for the name.

Enter a new name for this group.

You may keep all other information the same, or modify information to meet the needs of the new group.

You may create additional duplicate groups by clicking Add Another and renaming each duplicate group.

Click Create Group to create your subgroup(s) and return to the top level group.

To see the Subgroups for your current group, open your Dashboard, choose Subgroups from the Add dropdown, and drag the Subgroups: Active box onto your page.

You can also show a hierarchal tree of your Groups Below by dragging the Groups Below: Tree View box onto your page. See Show organization hierarchy tree for more information.

See Add boxes to your page for more information on adding elements to your page with your Dashboard.
