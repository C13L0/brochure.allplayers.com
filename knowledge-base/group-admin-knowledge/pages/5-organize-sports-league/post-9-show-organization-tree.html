As you build the groups needed for your organization, you can view these groups in a hierarchal tree display on a group's page. Adding a Groups Below: Tree View box to your page allows you to see the Subgroups under the group you are viewing. You can also easily access actions from your Groups Below: Tree View box, such as editing a group, broadcasting a message to a group or managing members of a group. Using our sample organization, we'll display the Groups Below our main entity, Springfield Recreation Center.

Navigate to the group page where you want to display the Groups Below, and open your Dashboard.

In the Add menu, choose Subgroups.

Click and drag the Groups Below: Tree View box onto your page.

See Add boxes to your page for more information on adding elements to your page with your dashboard.

When you have placed your new box where you would like it on your page, click Save in your Dashboard.

When you are logged in as an administrator to your group, you will see links associated with each group to allow you to easily jump to another group or perform actions for each group. Group members who do not have administrator privileges will not see these additional links.
