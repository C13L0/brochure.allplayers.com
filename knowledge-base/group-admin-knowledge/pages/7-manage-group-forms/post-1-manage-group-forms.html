As a group administrator, you can create forms to gather information from your group members.

To add, move or remove members in groups, go to the top level group page.More Tools > Create Form in your Group Toolbox.


Name your form.


Select the group to which you want the form to apply.

The default group is the group that you were on when you clicked the Create Form link. You can add any group that is a subgroup of this group by checking the box next to it.


Choose what fields you would like to include in the form. You may designate the fields as optional, required or leave them off the form entirely.

The fields are divided into categories. Click on the category to open up the section and select the fields you need.


Once you have finished selecting the fields, click Save and you're done.

You will be taken to your new form and you can now find your form under More Tools > Manage Forms in your Group Toolbox.


To remove members from groups, select Remove member(s) from group(s) from the pulldown menu.

Click Execute.
