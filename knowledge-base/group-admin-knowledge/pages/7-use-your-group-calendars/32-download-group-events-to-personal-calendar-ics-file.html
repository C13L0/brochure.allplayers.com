AllPlayers.com gives you the ability to download an .ics file from an AllPlayers.com group calendar to import into your electronic calendar application. The .ics file format is the file type required by most popular calendar applications to import the calendar.

To download your group's calendar:

Navigate to the group whose calendar you want to export.

Click Calendar under your group name.


Click the ical button above the calendar.

Download/save the .ics file to your desired location on your computer. Then follow the procedures required by your calendar application to import the .ics file.
