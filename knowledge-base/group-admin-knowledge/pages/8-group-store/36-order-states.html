As a group admin of a group store, you will see multiple "order states" throughout your store's different reports. These order states are powerful filter items that can make your life much easier when you are trying to find specific orders.

Pending

Pending

The pending state signifies that the purchaser has completed checkout but still needs to pay for the product. This is only applicable when the user checked out using the "pay in person" method.

Completed

Completed

The completed state signifies that the order has been completely paid for.

Cancelled

Cancelled

The cancelled state signifies that the order has been cancelled, and the purchaser is no longer liable to pay for the order.

Shopping cart

Shopping cart

The shopping cart state signifies that the product is in the purchasers shopping cart, but has not yet been payed for.

Checkout

Checkout

The checkout state signifies that the purchaser is currently in the process of checking out for the order.

Invoice

Invoice

The invoice state signifies that the item has been invoiced and still needs to be payed for.
