Easily export your member information in customizable lists as either .csv (comma-separated values) or .xls (excel) files.

To export member information, go to the top level group. Click More Tools > Group Settings in your Group Toolbox.


Using the search tool, you can narrow your results down by role.

Select the roles that you would like to display, then click Search. The new list will only display those roles that you selected.


Scroll to the bottom of the page and select either the .csv or .xls icon to start the export. Save your file to your desired location when prompted.
