Easily export your member information in customizable lists as either .csv (comma-separated values) or .xls (excel) files.

To export member information:

Navigate to the top level group.

In the group toolbox, navigate to Administrative Tools > Manage > Members.


To filter which members and which member information you would like exported, use the Filters on the left of the page.


You can learn more about the filter box in our manage members guide.


To start the export, on the bottom left of the page, select either the csv or xls icon. Save your file to your desired location when prompted.
