As a group administrator, you can easily assign and remove roles from your members.

To add, move or remove members in groups, go to the top level group page.More Tools > Group Settings in your Group Toolbox.


Members of your top level group display automatically.

You can refine your search to display members of any subgroup, and select which roles and fields you wish to display. Select the desired options and click Search.

NOTE: If you don’t see the group you were looking for you may not have selected a high enough group level.


Click the checkbox next to the members to which you want to add or remove roles.


If the members do not appear in the top level group, select Include Groups Below and add the relevant group.

Members added to groups will remain in any group of which they were already a member.

NOTE: If you don’t see the group you were looking for you may not have selected a high enough group level.


To give members additional roles, select Assign role(s) to member(s) within group(s)/b> from the pulldown menu.

Click Execute.


To remove roles from members, select Remove role(s) from member(s) within group(s) from the pulldown menu.

Click Execute.
