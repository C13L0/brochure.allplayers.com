AllPlayers.com gives you the ability to download a .csv file from an AllPlayers.com group calendar. CSV stands for comma-separated values. CSV files can typically be viewed in a text or spreadsheet application.

Navigate to the group whose calendar you want to export.

One way to navigate to a group is to click the Groups menu item in your personal menu bar, then select the group if it appears, or choose More to view all of your groups.

Click Calendar under your group name.

Scroll to the bottom of the calendar, and click the CSV icon located directly below the calendar.

Download/save the .csv file to your desired location on your computer. Then open the file in your desired application.
