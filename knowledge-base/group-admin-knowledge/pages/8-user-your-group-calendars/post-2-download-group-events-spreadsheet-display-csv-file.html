AllPlayers.com gives you the ability to download a .csv file from an AllPlayers.com group calendar. CSV stands for comma-separated values. CSV files can typically be viewed in a text or spreadsheet application.

To download a .csv file from your group's calendar:

Navigate to your group.

Click Calendar under your group's name.


Click the csv button above the calendar.

Download/save the .csv file to your desired location on your computer. Then open the file in your desired application.
