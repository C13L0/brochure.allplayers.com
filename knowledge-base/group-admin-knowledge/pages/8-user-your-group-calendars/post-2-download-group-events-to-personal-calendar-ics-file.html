AllPlayers.com gives you the ability to download a .ics file from an AllPlayers.com group calendar to import into your electronic calendar application. The .ics file format is the file type required by most popular calendar applications to import the calendar.

Navigate to the group whose calendar you want to export.

One way to navigate to a group is to click the Groups menu item in your personal menu bar, then select the group if it appears, or choose More to view all of your groups.

Click Calendar under your group name.

Scroll to the bottom of the calendar, and click the calendar icon located directly below the calendar.

Download/save the .ics file to your desired location on your computer. Then follow the procedures required by your calendar application to import the .ics file.
