Every group has a group calendar, where group administrators can post events and activities for the group. These events update and feed into members' personal calendars automatically.

To view your group's calendar:

Navigate to your group.

Click Calendar under your group's name.


If you do not see the calendar link for the group, you can enable this feature in your Group Toolbox at Administrative Tools > Features.


The Month View will appear, displaying any events that have been created by the group.

You can change the view between Month, Week, and Day using the buttons above the calendar.


You can navigate forward or backward through days, weeks, or months using the arrows above your group calendar.


To view upcoming events for the group in a list view, click Upcoming List.


To switch from a list view of upcoming events to the calendar view, click View.

Filter

You can use the filter to narrow down what events display in your group calendar.

Groups

This option allows you to specify which subgroups' events appear on the calendar.

Category

This option allows you to filter your results by the event category.

Location/Resource

This option allows you to filter your results by their locations and used resources.

Once you have chosen your options for filtering, click apply.
