Every group has a group calendar, where group administrators can post events and activities for the group. These events update and feed into members' personal calendar automatically.

Navigate to the group whose calendar you want to view.

One way to navigate to a group is to click the Groups menu item in your personal menu bar, then select the group if it appears, or choose More to view all of your groups.

Click Calendar under your group name.

×

If you do not see the calendar link for the group, you can enable this feature in your Group Toolbox at More Tools > Features.

The Month View will appear, displaying any events that have been created by the group.

You can view your group calendar by month, week or day.

You can navigate forward or backward through days, weeks or months using the arrow above your group calendar.

To view upcoming events for the group in a list view, click Upcoming List.

To switch from a list view of upcoming events to the calendar view, click View.

You can filter the events that display in your group calendar.

Click the Filter link. Any Groups Below the current group are available for filtering, as well as event category and locations/resources.

Choose your options for filtering, then click Apply.

×

To select multiple consecutive groups or categories, hold down the shift key on your keyboard. To select non-consecutive groups or categories, hold down the command key on a Mac, or control key on a PC.
