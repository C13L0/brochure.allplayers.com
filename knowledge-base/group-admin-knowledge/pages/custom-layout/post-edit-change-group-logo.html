To change your group logo, click More Tools > Group Settings in your Group Toolbox.


Remove and existing logo

Scroll to the Additional Options section of your Edit Group Settings page.

Click Remove.


Click Save Group to save and apply your changes.


Upload a new logo

Scroll to the Additional Options section of your Edit Group Settings page.

Click Choose File and select the new image you want to use from your computer.


Click Upload after you have selected your logo file.


Click Save Group to save and apply your changes.
