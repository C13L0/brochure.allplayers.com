As a group administrator, you can easily add, move or remove members in your groups using your Manage Members tools.

To add, move or remove members in groups, go to the top level group page.More Tools > Group Settings in your Group Toolbox.


Members of your top level group display automatically.

You can refine your search to display members of any subgroup, and select which roles and fields you wish to display. Select the desired options and click Search.

NOTE: If you donâ€™t see the group you were looking for you may not have selected a high enough group level.


Click the checkbox next to the members you want to change.


To add members to groups, select Add member(s) to group(s) from the pulldown menu.

Members added to groups will remain in any group of which they were already a member.

Click Execute.


To move members from one group to another, select Move member(s) to group(s) from the pulldown menu.

Click Execute.


To remove members from groups, select Remove member(s) from group(s) from the pulldown menu.

Click Execute.
