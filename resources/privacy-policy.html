Your privacy rights

AllPlayers.com is an open-source software platform that seamlessly links organizations and their membership together in one unified environment. Two of the many features most organizations utilize within the AllPlayers.com platform are our user registration service and member management solution. When organizations use the AllPlayers.com platform to connect with their membership it creates convenience for the organization and their members. Organizations have an enterprise cloud-based solution with security and stability to handle groups of any size, and their members have real-time access to information from the website or through our mobile apps.

We appreciate the trust these organizations place in AllPlayers.com, Inc. and we are committed to respecting the privacy and security of any information stored, collected and retrieved through their membership management solution. The AllPlayers.com platform only requires the following fields to create an account:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Email
  • Birthdate
  • Gender

Any other information collected or requested is organization specific and only accessible by that organization and you, and is not shared, distributed, sold or used for any marketing activity by AllPlayers.com. As with any website there is data that is collected and stored on your machine while you are utilizing the platform. The information below describes the data and how AllPlayers.com uses the information.

Information we collect

When you visit AllPlayers.com, you provide us with personal information that you knowingly choose to disclose through registration and website use information collected by us as you interact with our website.

AllPlayers.com gathers information about how visitors navigate through our website by using clickstream data gathered with cookies, a data file that is stored by the web browser on your computer, and/or other online tools. Cookies enable you to be recognized when you return to our website or place an order. They also allow us to enhance and personalize your online experience, so that the information you receive is more relevant to you.

As part of any team registration fee collection, fundraising donation, and product or service transaction, we may collect information necessary to process your requested action, including transaction information and credit card information. No credit card data is exposed, viewable or stored on the AllPlayers.com website. This information is passed directly to your organization’s merchant processor, and stored in their secure facility.

Any improper collection or misuse of information provided on AllPlayers.com is a violation of the terms of service and should be reported to customer support.

Using information we collect

Profile information is used by AllPlayers.com primarily to be presented back to and edited by you when you access the service. When you register for a group, the information collected during registration is presented to the appropriate organization within our member management solution, which you control through your privacy settings. In some cases where your privacy settings permit it (e.g., posting a comment to your profile page), other AllPlayers.com users may be able to add information to your profile, but you will always maintain control of your profile through your privacy settings and site features (e.g., delete comment feature). At AllPlayers.com, we always want to provide you with the best possible online experience and manage your transactions exactly as you've requested. In order to do this, we collect information that allows us to:

  • Process and track your transaction
  • Provide the services that you request and process payments in conjunction with these requests
  • Contact you about the status of a transaction
  • Improve our merchandise selection and customer service

Information we share with others and your privacy rights

AllPlayers.com, Inc. contracts with other companies to provide certain services, including credit card processing, shipping, and name and address verification. We provide these companies with only the information they need to perform their services and work closely with them to ensure that your privacy is respected and protected. These companies are prohibited by contract from using this information for their own marketing purposes or from sharing this information with anyone other than AllPlayers.com.

On rare occasions, we may disclose specific information upon governmental request, in response to a court order, or when required by law to do so. We may also share information with companies assisting in fraud protection or investigation. We do not provide information to these agencies or companies for marketing or commercial purposes.

Security

It is our intent to protect against the loss, misuse or alteration of information that we have collected from you or received from a partner. We use a variety of current technologies and processes for protection of our customer data. Examples of AllPlayers.com's security and privacy measures include: physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards; security monitoring tools; documented security policies; use of strong encryption for transmissions of sensitive data; restricted access of personally identifiable information; and periodic security audits by third party security experts.

When you create an account or make a purchase on our website, your transactional information is transmitted in a safe, encrypted format. AllPlayers.com is committed to privacy and security. AllPlayers.com is compliant with the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard ("PCI DSS") and has partnered with a Level 1 service provider for credit card transactions.

Privacy of children on our website

At AllPlayers.com, we recognize the need to provide additional privacy protections for children who visit our site.

  • AllPlayers.com is compliant with the federal Child Online Privacy and Protection Act (COPPA – http://www.ftc.gov/ogc/coppa1.htm).
  • Children under 13 years of age are prohibited from creating an account on AllPlayers.com.
  • Parents of children 13 years of age and younger have the ability to create an account for their child and manage the online environment for their children on AllPlayers.com.
  • Children 13 years of age and younger cannot enter any personally identifiable information on the AllPlayers.com website without their parent's expressed consent.
  • The user accounts of any child 13 years of age or younger that are created by parents are not included in the AllPlayers.com search engine results and can only be accessed according to the privacy settings configured by the parent.

Policy changes

We appreciate the trust you place in AllPlayers.com, Inc. and we are committed to respecting your privacy and the security of your personal information. In rare instances it may become necessary to change or update this policy document. AllPlayers.com reserves the right to make changes to this privacy statement. If our information practices change, we will post these changes on our website. We encourage you to review our privacy policy regularly.
